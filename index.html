Visualize and annotate pre-computed spectrograms and waveforms

Visualize & Listen

Load pre-computed spectrograms and waveforms and visualize them using synchronized zoom, pan, and audio playback.

Annotate & Analyze

Designed for the annotation of audio and spectrogram data, with support for custom data types.
